Hackslash

Welcome to Hackslash. This competition revolves around an IT company, Insecure Inc., trying to secure their networks. It's your job to try and hack their website.

Rules

  • The competition will last for 60 minutes.
  • You will be awarded 10 points for completing each level.
  • There will be a penalty of 5 points for using the hints.
  • The team with the most points wins.
  • In case of a tie, the team completing more number of levels will be the winner.

NOTE : Please do not close the browser window or hit the back button during the competition or you risk losing your data.
