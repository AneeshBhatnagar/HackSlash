Level 4

Insecure Inc. has organized a poll for Employee of the Month in which each nominee is rated on a scale of 5 and the final rating is calculated using the average of all the votes given.

It seems that one candidate has unscrupulously increased his rating to an impossible value. Your friend Harry, who is one of the nominees, has requested you to increase his rating to a value greater than that of John. Can you pull it off?
